# Changelog

## Known Issues

- Occasional loud audio pop on the very first preview when loading YouTube
- Rarely, hovering quickly between thumbnails can cause two previews to play audio simultaneously
- May need a page refresh immediately after installing the extension

## [1.7]
- Tried to fix preview volume not working — made things worse, reverted to 1.5

## [1.6]
- Attempted fixes for loud audio pops and edge cases — broke the extension entirely

## [1.5]
- Reduced loud beeps and volume bursts at the start of previews
- Faster and more reliable preview detection

## [1.4]
- YouTube Shorts no longer affected by the extension

## [1.3]
- Volume slider changes now apply instantly without refreshing the page

## [1.2]
- Fixed previews being missed
- More reliable detection across the page

## [1.1]
- Fixed extension accidentally affecting the main video player

## [1.0] - Initial Release
- Automatically lowers preview video volume (default: 20%)
- Adjustable slider from 0–100%
- Settings saved between sessions
